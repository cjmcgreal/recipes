## Ingredients
- **1 lb** of sweet potato
- **2 tbl** of Herb de Provence
- **0.25 cup** of olive oil
- **1 pinch** of salt and pepper, to taste
## Tools
- Peeler
- Cutting Board
- Knife
- Mixing Bowl
- Oven Pan
## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Peel Sweet Potato
3. Chop Sweet Potato
4. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
5. Roast for 20 min
6. Pull out and stir
7. Roast for 20 min
